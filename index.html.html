Game Rules

Yugonostalgia is a card game designed for entertainment and education. It is inspired by the nostalgia of Yugoslavia and the legendary football clubs from the former Yugoslav republics. Here are the basic rules:

  1. Players begin with a shuffled deck of cards.
  2. The goal is to organize all cards into their respective suits in ascending order.
  3. You can move cards between columns if the sequence and colors allow.
  4. Empty spaces can only be filled with a King.
  5. Use the draw pile to access more cards if no moves are available.
  6. The game ends when all suits are completed or no more moves are possible.

Enjoy the Yugonostalgia while challenging your strategic thinking and reliving memories of Yugoslavian heritage!
